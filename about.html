About Us

"For the Students By the Students"

Introduction

Functions as the voice of students in Swinburne Sarawak.As the voice of the student, SSSC reaches to students and get feedbacks to the management so that student experience on campus is improved. Furthermore, SSSC members also acts as volunteers, assisting in events and activities organized by the management and clubs. SSSC also plans and organizes our own events such as prom night, Swinburne Carnival, Cultural Night and many more.Our main mission is to provide a bridge for communication between students and the management so that student life on campus can be improved which will enrich the student experience on campus.


Our Objective

Further the best interests of its members as part of the University's provision of student services. Bridge cultural gaps that may exist among students from various religious and cultural backgrounds.


Our Job

We are the only student representative and as mentioned before, we are doing our best to enhance Swinburne students experience. However, besides making sure that you are a student get what you have paid for, we also organize events, oversee and manage all the clubs and definitely, we listen to the students complaints and feedback.


Our Function

Improve student's experience by promoting common interests of students studying in Swinburne Sarawak and coordinating activities of student clubs. Build a sense of pride, identity and unity amongst student community, and promote togetherness regardless of race, religion and culture. Be the recognised representative channel between students and the University and any other internal or external body as provided for by the University.


Our Location

Our office is located in Swinburne Sarawak campus, Building L2 (Also Known As The Chillin Crib), Second floor.
